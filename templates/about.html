Welcome to {{ info.name }}

{{ info.name }} is a premier Design & Deploy hackathon organized by the Mechanical Engineering Division at Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences. This event brings together creative minds, innovative thinkers, and passionate developers from across the region. This 3-day intensive event challenges participants to design, develop, and deploy innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Primary Concept: Teams are required to design or demonstrate a product that is both feasible and innovative. The focus is on creating practical solutions that can be realistically implemented, showcasing creativity alongside technical viability.

Whether you're a seasoned developer, a design enthusiast, or someone just starting their journey in tech, {{ info.name }} provides the perfect platform to showcase your skills, learn from experts, and collaborate with talented individuals.
